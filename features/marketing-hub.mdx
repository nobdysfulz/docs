The Marketing Hub (`/MarketingHub`) provides a centralized marketing management platform with a content calendar, campaign tracking, template library, and AI content generation.

## Page Layout

The Marketing Hub uses an internal tab system (shadcn `Tabs` component) rather than the `ContextualTopNav`:
- **Overview** - Dashboard with today's focus, content calendar, and recent content
- **AI Creator** - Full AI content generator
- **Recents** - All recently generated content
- **Preferences** - Content generation settings

## Overview Tab

### Quick Access Cards

Two navigation cards at the top:
- **Templates** - Links to `/MarketingTemplates` for browsing social post templates
- **Campaigns** - Links to `/MarketingCampaigns` for campaign management

### Today's Focus (Left Column)

Shows what's on the agenda today:

#### Daily Template
If a `TaskTemplate` with `category: 'social_media'` and `triggerType: 'day_of_week'` matches today's day:
- Displays the template title and description
- **Copy** button to clipboard
- **Generate** button to create AI content from the template topic

#### Calendar Items
Items from the marketing calendar for today:
- Social posts with Canva template links
- Email campaigns in progress
- Active campaign steps with progress indicators

#### Active Campaigns
Current campaigns with:
- Campaign name and current step
- Link to view full campaign details

### Content Calendar (Right Column)

A full monthly calendar (`MonthlyCalendar` component) showing:
- **Template markers** - Blue badges for recurring social media templates by day of week
- **Calendar entries** - Purple badges for scheduled content items
- **Today highlight** - Purple border on current date
- **Click to add** - Click any date to create a new calendar entry
- **Entry details** - Click entries to edit via `CalendarEntryModal`
- **Add Event** button for creating new calendar entries

Calendar data is fetched via the `marketing/getCalendarMonth` backend function.

### Recent Content (Left Column)

Shows the last 3 generated content items with:
- Content type badge
- Title (truncated)
- Creation date
- Click to view full content in `ContentDetailModal`

## AI Creator Tab

Embeds the `AIContentGenerator` component for full content generation:
- Topic selection and customization
- Platform targeting
- Tone and length preferences
- Market intelligence integration
- Generated content preview and save

## Recents Tab

The `RecentGenerated` component shows all generated content:
- Full list with type, title, and date
- Click to view in detail modal
- Limited to last 20 items

## Preferences Tab

Content generation defaults:
- **Default Tone** - Professional, casual, educational, promotional
- **Default Length** - Short, medium, long
- Save button persists to `ContentPreference` entity

## AI Content Generation from Calendar

When generating content from a calendar template:

1. Finds the `content_studio_social_post` prompt config
2. Combines template title and description as the topic
3. Injects market intelligence data (if available) as context
4. Calls `openaiChat` with GPT-4o (max 1500 tokens, temperature 0.7)
5. Saves result to `GeneratedContent` entity
6. Refreshes the recent content list

## Calendar Entry Management

The `CalendarEntryModal` supports:
- Creating new entries on specific dates
- Editing existing entries
- Linking to post templates, email templates, or campaigns
- Deleting entries
- Refreshing calendar data after changes

## Data Sources

| Entity | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `GeneratedContent` | Stored AI-generated content |
| `ContentPreference` | User's default generation settings |
| `AiPromptConfig` | AI prompt templates |
| `TaskTemplate` | Recurring content schedule by day |
| `MarketIntelligence` | Market data for content context |
| `PostTemplate` | Social post templates |
| `EmailTemplate` | Email templates |
| `MarketingCampaign` | Campaign definitions |
